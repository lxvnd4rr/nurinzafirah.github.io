Learnify is a mobile learning application developed to help students manage their learning materials more effectively through digital flashcards. It enables creation, organization, study, and sharing of flashcards to support structured and engaging revision. The system also supports authentication, cloud sync, and local storage for seamless learning.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Learnify aligns with SDG 4 by improving access to educational resources, encouraging active learning through flashcards, supporting self-paced study, and enabling collaborative sharing among students.
